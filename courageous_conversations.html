Opportunities for Growth

Courageous Conversatons is an online social gathering of women, of all races, coming together to talk about race.

  • Listen as women share different perspectives on books and experiences
  • Learn practical ways in which to initiate and engage in these types of discussions
  • Contemplate our individual responsibilities to our communities
  • Be a part of weekly conversations around current topics
  • Challenge yourself to engage with individuals who differ from you outside of the group

How to Join

Enroll below to receive information regarding our upcoming Corageous Conversations.

Why to Join

We believe conversations like these, between you and me, are pivotal in our need to create an open dialogue on the topic of racial and social injustice.

Who can Join

We need to hear from women of all races, religions and different ages. All of our individual perspectives are valued in this space.



Message from our Online Facilatator

I'm often asked by white women, what can I do? And I think the first thing to do is listen and engage in conversations with people of color. Corageous Conversations was created to bring women of all colors together to break the silence in regard to race and inequality.
