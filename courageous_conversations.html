Opportunities for Growth

Courageous Conversations℠ is an online social gathering of women, of all races, coming together to talk about race.

  • Listen as women share different perspectives on books and experiences
  • Learn practical ways in which to initiate and engage in these types of discussions
  • Contemplate our individual responsibilities to our communities
  • Be a part of weekly conversations around current topics
  • Challenge yourself to engage with individuals who differ from you outside of the group

Who Can Join

We need to hear from women of all races, religions and different ages. All of our individual perspectives are valued in this space.

Why to Join

We believe conversations like these, between you and me, are pivotal in our need to create an open dialogue on the topic of racial and social injustice.

What is White Fragility?

In this book, antiracist educator Robin DiAngelo deftly illuminates the phenomenon of white fragility and allows us to understand racism as a practice not restricted to bad people. Referring to the defensive moves that white people make when challenged racially, white fragility is characterized by emotions such as anger, fear, and guilt, and by behaviors including argumentation and silence. These behaviors, in turn, function to reinstate white racial equilibrium and prevent any meaningful cross-racial dialogue. In this in-depth exploration, DiAngelo examines how white fragility develops, how it protects racial inequality, and what we can do to engage more constructively.
