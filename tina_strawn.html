Adopting A Life of Purpose & Servitude

For the past few years, all I've really known is that I want to teach, write, speak and travel. My fitness classes are incredibly important to me and I believe that teaching is a huge part of my purpose. But I also knew that my heart for activism and social and racial justice work would play a part in my purpose as well. I just haven't always known what that would look like, until now.

Satya Yoga Trips

The Satya Yoga Trips are just the beginning of more work that will eventually include anti-racism yoga workshops, trainings, and other forms of learning and education that will include diversity and inclusion training. This is work that I have already been doing, some of which you have seen on my social media, and things like my voter registration drives and teaching themed classes around anti-racism and social justice work.

I have also begun to branch out more and have agreed to speak at local high schools for their GSA (Gay Straight Alliance) and Diversity groups. The more we use our voices, the more doors of opportunity will open for us. For me, Satya Yoga Trips is just the framework of much more work to come.

There are only two mistakes that one can make along the road to truth, not going all the way, and not starting.
-Buddha

I am inviting you to be a part of this with me in whatever capacity you are interested and willing to do so. I am asking this of you because you because this work is required of all human beings. I also ask that you hold space for me as I embark on this new journey and take on the privilege and responsibility of walking through this anti-racism work with other women.
